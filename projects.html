89.5 FM WSOU

The Loudest Station in the Nation

89.5 FM WSOU is a modern active rock station serving the New York and New Jersey area. They are credited with giving some of the first-ever airplay to bands such as My Chemical Romance and Smashing Pumpkins, but now play metal. Kai DeJesus- or DJ DJ, as they're known on air- was their social media manager as well as their DJ.
