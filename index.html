who is kai?

Hi, my name is Kai and I use they/them pronouns. I love watercolors, board games, rock music, and above all, telling stories-- both true and fiction. But do you know what makes it better? Bringing people together while doing it!

I’m a soon-to-be graduate of Seton Hall University, with a Bachelors in Journalism. While there, I interned with Intuit Mailchimp, Slice of Culture, and Vurbl. I also worked to create content with The Gaming Sector, Seton Hall’s gaming club, and 89.5 WSOU, Seton Hall’s modern active rock station where I worked as DJ and social media manager.

In my spare time I love playing tabletop roleplaying games such as Dungeons and Dragons, as well as Technoir and City of Mist. I also love to write fiction and draw, some of which you can see in my mini-zines. But whether I’m telling a story with my friends or spinning music for alternative subcultures, I’m always set on bringing people together with what I do.

So let’s put our heads together! I can’t wait to see what we can make.
